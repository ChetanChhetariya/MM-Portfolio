How I got here

Mitasha Mehta is an advertising creative shaped by design, driven by strategy, and grounded in storytelling.

01

Foundation in Design

With a creative foundation rooted in art, craft, and hands-on making, my journey has always been guided by curiosity. I began my formal training in Interior Design, where I developed structure and discipline, but soon realized my interest lay beyond form—in meaning, narrative, and communication.

02

Creative Exploration

After completing my undergraduate degree, I returned to creativity at its core. I trained in 52 different art forms, earned multiple certifications, and became a certified mandala artist. I later launched my own design practice, creating marketing posters and promotional material for clients.

03

Strategic Shift

This experience shifted my focus from visual execution to how ideas connect with people. Wanting to understand advertising from the ground up, I pursued a Master's in Advertising at the Savannah College of Art and Design's School of Business Innovation.

04

Today

Today, I am especially drawn to projects that blend cultural insight with storytelling, believing that the most powerful ideas come from understanding people, traditions, and context. My work balances design craft, advertising strategy, and narrative thinking to create ideas that feel intentional, relevant, and human.
